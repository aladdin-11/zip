Our Methodology

The CancerAI Predictor uses machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of clinical cases to identify patterns in gene expression data and clinical markers that correlate with cancer development.

Key Biomarkers

  • PIK3CA expression
  • HER2 expression
  • TP53 mutations
  • KRAS mutations
  • EGFR expression

Clinical Factors

  • Age
  • Smoking status
  • Pack years
  • Family history
